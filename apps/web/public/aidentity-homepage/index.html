FAQs

Our frequently asked questions

You've got questions. We've got answers.

What does Aidentity do?

Aidentity adds an AI layer inside your SaaS so users can complete tasks through chat or voice instead of manually navigating the interface.

Who is Aidentity for?

Aidentity is built for SaaS companies that want to make their product AI-native without rebuilding their existing architecture.

What can users do with Aidentity?

Agents get accountable real-world capabilities without a browser assistant or customer-site embed.

Do we need to rebuild our product to use Aidentity?

No. Aidentity plugs into your existing product, indexes your interface, APIs, pages, and actions, then turns them into AI-powered workflows.

How is Aidentity different from a chatbot?

Chatbots answer questions. Aidentity helps users actually do things: navigate the product, execute actions, and automate repeated workflows.
